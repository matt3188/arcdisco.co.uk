Your Wedding Day

Your Wedding day will be one of the happiest days of your life, an opportunity to have the perfect day. We aim to provide a professional, dependable and personal service. Giving you the opportunity to have your wedding entertainment the way you want it.

It will be our goal to provide your choice of music for your special day.
If you don’t like flashing lights we can provide colourful wash lighting for your evening.
We can also provide modern L.E.D. uplighters to give the walls of your venue a wash of colour, changing the look and feel of your venue to match your chosen colour scheme.

It can be of great benefit to ask us about our early setup. When timings are tight or access to the venue is limited, this can be combined with our background music service, it can save time and hassle later in the day when getting ready for the evening’s entertainment.

Our wedding disco is modern, well maintained neat and tidy.

We aim to exceed your expectations – after all, your event is our reputation.
